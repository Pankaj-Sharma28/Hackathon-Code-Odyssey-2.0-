About the Event

Hackathon - Code Odyssey 2.0 is organized by IIC Cell & Department of BCA at IIMT College of Management, Greater Noida. It’s a platform for young innovators to showcase their problem-solving skills through coding and teamwork.

Event Theme

The hackathon focuses on Innovation in Technology with projects spanning across:

  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • Web & Mobile App Development
  • Cybersecurity & Blockchain
  • Smart Solutions for Education, Health & Environment

Event Schedule

  • 📌 Inauguration: 9:30 AM - 10:00 AM
  • 💻 Coding Round 1: 10:00 AM – 12:30 PM
  • 💻 1st Judgement: 12:30 AM – 12:45 PM
  • 💻 Coding Round 2: 12:45 PM – 3:45 PM
  • 💻 2nd Judgement: 3:45 PM – 4:00 PM
